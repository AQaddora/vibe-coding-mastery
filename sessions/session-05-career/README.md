## The promise

Career leverage and side-project compounding without burnout. A system that runs while you sleep — job alerts that don't waste your time, content that keeps publishing when you don't, a network that grows without you remembering to "engage", and a side project that ships one meaningful thing per month, every month, for a year.

## The trap discussed

The "always be shipping" cult. Burnout from sprinting on a side project alongside a full-time job, with no system to slow the throttle when life happens.

- The **Career Automation Stack** — five systems that run on autopilot
