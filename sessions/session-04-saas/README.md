# Session 4 — Build Trusted SaaS: Security, UX & Margin From Day 1

> *"The gap between a demo and a real business is three things — security, UX, and unit economics. Most AI-built SaaS dies because the founder thinks of those last."*

**Status:** ⏳ In production
**Runtime:** 30 minutes (target)
**Cost:** Free — like every session in this series

## The promise

What separates a demo from a real business. The minimum non-negotiables for any SaaS you sell — even at $9/mo. Multi-tenancy that actually isolates. Auth that AI didn't write from memory. UX that respects the first 30 seconds. Unit economics that survive your first 100 customers.

## The trap discussed

AI-generated auth, leaked API keys, broken multi-tenancy, and pricing pulled from your gut. Every one of these has killed a SaaS that had paying customers.

## What you walk away with

- The **Trusted SaaS Day 1 checklist** — what must be true before you take a dollar
- A **multi-tenancy threat model** template
- A **pricing experiment design** — three prices, three audiences, two weeks
- Doctrine entries covering SaaS security patterns
