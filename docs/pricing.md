# There's no pricing page

Everything in this repo — the **Mindset Foundations** tier of the Vibe Coding Mastery program — is **free, forever**.

There is no paid bundle, no upsell, no funnel attached to this material. It exists as a give to the developer community.

## What's free

- All 5 sessions of the **Mindset Foundations** series — slides, practical walkthroughs, assignments, references, terminology — published as production completes
- The full **PURE + AI Doctrine skill suite** — `doctrine-keeper`, `prime`, `understand`, `refine`, `execute`, `organize-agents`, `pure-orchestrator`, `handoff` — MIT licensed
- All **templates** for wiring your doctrine into Claude, Cursor, Copilot, Codex, Gemini CLI, and Windsurf
- All **tools** for scaffolding new sessions and building slides

## What about the more advanced Mastery tiers?

The Vibe Coding Mastery program is multi-tier. **Mindset Foundations** is tier 1 — free, opinionated, and the load-bearing layer under everything that comes after. More advanced tiers (deeper builds, full-stack AI products, monetization patterns) are in production. Pricing and timing for those will be announced **when they're ready** — never before.

If a tier costs money, it'll say so clearly on its own page. This tier doesn't.
