# There's no pricing page

Everything in this repo is **free, forever** — the 5-session mindset series and the open-source skill suite alike.

There is no paid bundle, no upsell, no funnel attached to this material. It exists as a give to the developer community.

## What's free

- All 5 sessions of the **mindset series** — slides, practical walkthroughs, assignments, references, terminology — published as production completes
- The full **PURE + AI Doctrine skill suite** — `doctrine-keeper`, `prime`, `understand`, `refine`, `execute`, `organize-agents`, `pure-orchestrator`, `handoff` — MIT licensed
- All **templates** for wiring your doctrine into Claude, Cursor, Copilot, Codex, Gemini CLI, and Windsurf
- All **tools** for scaffolding new sessions and building slides

## Stay in the loop

Mailing list at [aqaddoura.com](https://aqaddoura.com) — get a heads-up when Sessions 2–5 ship and when the longer paid program is ready.
